Axe: For all kinds of primary and advanced purposes, the axe is used and employed for agricultural operations. The Axe is used to shape out, cut, harvest timber and split out the huge logs. The sharp edge of the axe helps to easily cut the heavy and thick woods. Rake: Rake is the horticultural tool used and employed for outsider uses, in simple words it is the brush or broom used for outdoor operations. Pruning Saw: To trim the trees and unrequired shrubs, a pruning saw is widely used. All varieties of Pruning saw are available in different sizes and shapes, but all of them have a hard point and heat-treated teeth. Land Mover: It is also known as the mover, grasser or lawn mower that is supposed to clean the entire farmland.
